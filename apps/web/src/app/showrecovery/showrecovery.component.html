Account Backup and Recovery

This is your account recovery word pattern. This pattern is the only way to recover your account and decrypt your data if you lose access to all your Quick Crypt passkeys. These words are shown only once and cannot be displayed again, so copy and store them now.

  1. Save all of these words in the exact order they appear
  2. Store this pattern in a secure and durable location, separate from your passkeys
  3. Do not allow untrusted parties to access your recovery pattern

Although someone with access to your recovery words cannot decrypt your data, they could delete your Quick Crypt account. We recommend printing a physical copy of your recovery words and storing the printout in a lockbox or safe. If you lose both your passkeys and these recovery words, you will permanently lose access to your encrypted data.
