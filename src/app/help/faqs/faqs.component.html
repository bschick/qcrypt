  • Can I used Quick Crypt to send encrypted information to other people?

    • Quick Crypt was design for encytpion, storage, and decryption of your own data rather than for sharing of secure data. Data can only be decrypted by someone who has both the password used for encryption and the same Quick Crypt Passkey. While Passkeys are not designed for sharing, they certainly could be shared. So although not design for secret sharing, you can accomplish that by exporing the Quick Crypt Passkey from your Passkey managment tool and sharing that with an intended recipient.
