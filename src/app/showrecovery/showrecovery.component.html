Save Your Recovery Link

You must save this recovery link securely and separately from your passkeys if possible. If your Quick Crypt passkeys are lost or damaged, this link is the only way to restore your ability to decrypt data. You can also use this link to decrypt data with the Quick Crypt command-line tool.

Keep this recovery link private. While another person with this link cannot decrypt your data without the passwords you use during encryption, they could delete your Quick Crypt account making it less convenient to decrypt your data. So be sure to keep this link private.
