Save Your Recovery Link

It is important that you save this recovery link in a secure location, seperate from your passkeys. If your Quick Crypt passkeys are lost or damaged, this link is the only way to restore your abilty decrypt data you previously encypted (you also need your encryption passwords).

Do not share this recovery link with anyone. While another person with this link can not decrypt your data without also having your encryption passwords (and the encypted data itself), someone else could use this link to remove your ability to decrypt previously encypted data.

So make sure you keep this link private.
